## Overview

This document defines the investable universe for the 
Hypersonic Defense Screener. Inclusion requires a company 
to meet ALL of the following criteria:

1. Publicly traded on a US exchange (NYSE or NASDAQ)
2. Meaningful revenue exposure to hypersonics, next-gen 
   defense, or critical supply chain components thereof
3. Operates at the picks-and-shovels layer — not a 
   prime contractor
4. Sufficient liquidity for retail position sizing
5. Headquartered in the United States

---

## Inclusion Criteria Detail

### What Qualifies
- Specialized component or subsystem manufacturers
- Advanced materials and specialty alloys suppliers
- Propulsion system manufacturers (non-prime)
- Additive manufacturing for defense/aerospace applications
- Autonomous systems hardware manufacturers
- Guidance, navigation, and control subsystem suppliers
- Test and range infrastructure providers
- Electronic components and subsystems for defense platforms

### What Disqualifies
- Prime contractors (LMT, RTX, NOC, GD, BA)
- Pure software or cybersecurity companies
- International defense companies
- Consumer or commercial aerospace without defense exposure
- Companies with hypersonics/next-gen defense revenue 
  below meaningful threshold

### Removal Triggers
- Acquisition by a prime contractor
- Delisting or going private
- Revenue mix shifts away from defense below threshold
- Fundamental thesis violation (e.g. loss of key contracts)
- Liquidity falls below minimum threshold
- Federal investigation into foreign adversary control, 
  infiltration, or ownership — regardless of resolution 
  status. National security risk and contract 
  vulnerability are disqualifying independent of 
  legal outcome.
