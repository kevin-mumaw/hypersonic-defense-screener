## Overview

This document defines the investable universe for the 
Hypersonic Defense Screener. Inclusion requires a company 
to meet ALL of the following criteria:

1. Publicly traded on a US exchange (NYSE or NASDAQ)
2. Meaningful revenue exposure to next-generation defense 
   technology or critical supply chain components thereof
3. Headquartered in the United States
4. Sufficient liquidity for retail position sizing
5. Passes the geographic safety gate (US/Canada domicile)

---

## Inclusion Criteria Detail

### What Qualifies
- DoD AI/software command and control platforms
- Autonomous and unmanned systems manufacturers
- Hypersonic propulsion and thermal protection suppliers
- Space launch and strategic missile infrastructure
- Advanced materials and specialty alloys suppliers
- Defense electronics and harsh-environment subsystems
- IoMT sensors, tactical networks, and drone orchestration
- Additive manufacturing for defense/aerospace applications
- Precision aerospace component manufacturers

### What Disqualifies
- Prime contractors (LMT, RTX, NOC, GD, BA) unless 
  a documented next-gen technology unit qualifies as 
  a special case exception
- Pure software/cyber plays without defense hardware exposure
- International defense companies
- Consumer or commercial aerospace without defense exposure

### Removal Triggers
- Acquisition by a prime contractor
- Delisting or going private
- Revenue mix shifts away from defense below threshold
- Fundamental thesis violation (e.g. loss of key contracts)
- Liquidity falls below minimum threshold
- Federal investigation into foreign adversary control, 
  infiltration, or ownership — regardless of resolution 
  status
- Cash runway below 2 years for unprofitable companies

### Tier Definitions
- **Disruptor:** High-growth, software-first or pure-play 
  next-gen defense. R&D > 10% of revenue or revenue 
  growth > 15% YoY. Target weight: 15% per position.
- **Standard:** Established defense supply chain with 
  meaningful next-gen exposure. Target weight: 10% per position.
- **Anchor:** Diversified or prime-adjacent. Target weight: 
  5% per position.

- Standard: Monthly
- Shock trigger: If any position exceeds 13.5% or 
  falls below 6.5% of total portfolio, force rebalance

### Position Sizing by Score
| Score | Signal | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 75-100 | STRONG | Consider adding — up to tier maximum |
| 50-74 | NEUTRAL | Hold / monitor |
| 25-49 | WEAK | Consider trimming |
